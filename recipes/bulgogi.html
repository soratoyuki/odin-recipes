Preston's Culturally Appropriated Bulgogi!

The Background

What actually is bulgogi? I have no idea. But I won't let me own lack of knowledge prevent me from whitemansplaining this dish to improve my search engine optimization.

It's just stir-fried beef with some sauce right?

The Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Bulgogi sauce
  • Olive oil

The Instructions

  1. Add olive oil to a pan on high heat
  2. Add a bunch of beef
  3. Add some sauce to the pan
  4. Stir it around until you think it's probably done.
  5. It's beef. What are you going to do, undercook it? Lol.
  6. Add it to a staging bowl.
  7. Add more beef and sauce to the pan.
  8. Repeat until you're out of beef and sauce.
  9. Eat it
